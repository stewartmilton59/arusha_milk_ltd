About Us

Serving Since 2012

Our Story

From the slopes of Mount Meru, we bring pure, farm-fresh milk to Arusha.

Arusha Milk Products Ltd started as a small family dairy. Today we are trusted by hundreds of hotels, cafes and households. We work with local farmers to deliver unadulterated, hygienic milk and dairy products every morning.
